I adopted a bunny from the Toronto Humane Society in November. Her name is Ruby and her first birthday was December 22nd, 2015. (We have the same birthday!) Here are some fun facts I discovered while researching bunnies.

  • Bunnies are crepuscular. They are active primarily at dawn and dusk. Ruby likes to lie around for most of the day, but in the evening she does Bunny Nascar.
  • Bunnies have so much power in their legs that, if frightened, they can make motions powerful enough to break their own spine. You shouldn't pick a bunny up if she doesn't want you to.
  • Bunnies should not eat only (or even mainly) carrots. Their diet consists mostly of hay.
  • Bunnies often sleep with their eyes open. Supposedly this helps them be on the alert for predators.
