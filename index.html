My partner and I adopted a bunny from the Toronto Humane Society. Her name is Ruby and she will be 1 year old on December 22nd, 2015. Here are some fun facts we discovered while researching our new pet.

  • Bunnies are crepuscular. That means they are active primarily at dawn and dusk. Ruby likes to lie around for most of the day, but in the evening she turns the living room into Bunny Nascar.
  • Bunnies have so much power in their legs that, if frightened, they can make motions powerful enough to break their own spine. So, you really shouldn't pick a bunny up if she doesn't want to let you.
  • Bunnies should not eat only (or even mostly) carrots
